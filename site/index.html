The scene & date nights

  • 💃 Ecstatic Dance Maui — Sundays ~12:30–3:15 PM ($20), but in Haʻikū/Kahului (40–55 min drive), not Kīhei. Check @mauiecstaticdance for the venue (Jul 27 & Aug 3).
  • 🔥 Luau — Te Au Moana (Wailea, several nights/wk) or Old Lāhainā Lūʻau (nightly, north Lahaina — open and welcoming visitors).
  • 🪘 Obon / Bon dances — free taiko + open dance circle, matches your vibe; Wailuku falls ~Jul 31–Aug 1. Live music at the MACC amphitheater + ProArts Kīhei — check their calendars for your dates.
  • 🌅 Best sunsets (free) — Keawakapu (your beach), the 1.5-mi Wailea Beach Path past five crescent beaches, and quiet Polo Beach.
  • 💆 Couples massage — Maui's Best Massage (Kīhei), side-by-side from ~$260 — a great birthday-morning move; or a private sunset sail-and-snorkel for two.

Weather & what to pack

  • ☀️ Sunny & dry — highs ~87°F, nights ~72°F, ocean ~78–80°F. One of the driest months (~9% of days see rain, usually brief). Sunset ~7:00 PM (book the birthday table ~6:15).
  • 🧴 Reef-safe sunscreen is the LAW — only mineral (zinc oxide / titanium dioxide) is legal, fines to $1,000. UV is extreme — pack mineral SPF 30–50 from home (cheaper than on-island).
  • 🌊 Peak south-swell — south-facing beaches can get high surf; snorkel calm mornings, check the day-of surf advisory.
  • ❄️ A/C optional — steady trade winds off the water keep 72°F nights comfortable; the pick has split A/C anyway.
